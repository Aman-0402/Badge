# Quickdraw

## What It Is
Awarded for closing an issue or pull request within 5 minutes of opening it. One-time badge, no tiers.

## How to Earn It

1. Open an issue (or pull request) in any repo you have access to
2. **Within 5 minutes**, close it
3. Done — badge appears within a few hours

## Step by Step

1. Go to any repo → **Issues** tab → **New issue**
2. Add any title (e.g. `Test issue`)
3. Click **Submit new issue**
4. Immediately click **Close issue** on the same page

**The 5-minute clock starts the moment you submit.**

## Tips

- Do it on your own repo to avoid bothering maintainers of other repos
- Closing as "not planned" or "completed" both count
- PRs also work — open and close a PR within 5 minutes

## Common Mistakes

- Waiting too long after opening before closing — must be under 5 minutes
- Opening on a repo where you don't have permission to close issues
