# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project is open and welcoming to everyone. We pledge to make participation a harassment-free experience regardless of age, background, experience level, or identity.

## Our Standards

**Positive behavior:**
- Being respectful and constructive in feedback
- Helping newcomers get started
- Accepting different approaches and opinions

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Dismissing contributions without explanation
- Any form of discrimination

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported by opening a private issue or contacting the maintainer at **think.like.ai.aman@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
